The Coast is Yours

Breathtaking views, serene walks on the beach, calming sounds of the ocean - all in a stay at Ocean Creek.

Unique and Comfortable

We offer rooms and suites designed to encourage a getaway state of mind. Inspired by poems, the Blossom and West Wind rooms both have jetted tubs, one with an ocean view. The Seafarer and Heritage suites each offer a unique experience with walk-in rainfall showers. Relax in luxuriously appointed queen beds and custom seating. The suites have gas fireplaces that take any chill out of the air.

Your host and hostess are on-site to help make your stay memorable.

Year-round Destination

Each season on the south central Oregon coast has its own pleasures. Cool down in the summer with sunny, warm days and the breath of a breeze from the ocean. Revel in the continuing warm and sunny days of autumn. Watch the beauty and fury of storms in the winter, and enjoy the return of spring with wild flowers, ebbing tides and cleansing showers of liquid sunshine.

The small village of Yachats offers an impressive array of restaurants, regular entertainment, quaint shopping and beautiful, broad beaches. Walk many of the nature trails or visit spectacular Cape Perpetua, about a mile away.

Loaded with Luxery

Start your experience with ocean, forest, and creek views on an acre and a half tucked into a valley with wooded hillsides behind. Come into the gathering room facing the ocean with two walls of windows and a comfy place to sit with your coffee and have your specialty breakfast each morning. Luxuriate in your bath with natural bath products and enjoy your private deck. Visit the cozy sitting room on the second floor to read, do computer work, or just be alone.

Free Wi Fi and cable television are available in each room and suite. Three rooms include a microwave.
